# Race Locked

> Theorycrafting a new unique playthrough of World of Warcraft (classic/anniversary)
>
> Since Xaryu is well known for his PvP ability, lean heavily into a duelling focused event.
>
> Organisation & Preparation is key. Do not start or share the event until it is completely planned out, from team members to checkpoint events.

---

## Objective

- Be the guild to score the highest amount of points by the end of the event.
- The event ends when Molten Core is completed by a Guild (Full guild raid required).

## Motivation

- This is a fun twist on the usual objective of WoW, which is to reach the end game and complete the raid.
- Players are more focused on the leveling phase and interactions with the rival guild.
  - Making this fun and enjoyable from level 1.
  - Reducing end game fomo and making everyone be a part of the experience right away.
  - Thus, pulling in more fans/viewers/players.
- This is a truly unique and creative experience, which will likely draw in a big crowd of viewers and streamers, pushing Xaryu's position on Twitch from the good guy of WoW to the trailblazer in creative WoW content (if desired - this won't be the last big idea)
- Raise funds for charity.
  - Not only is this event to bring entertainment to followers and players, but for a good cause that players will be happy to contribute to.
  - Raising stakes in the competition as there is a real reward for the winner.
  - Consider a childrens hospital as the charity of choice (as a father, this would appeal to your contributors)

### Adjustments for "The next big WoW event"

The two guilds should be 'filled' with streamers. 20+ on each side would be ideal. Big streamers evenly spread between the two.

Several supporter guilds will be filled with the fans of the streamers.  
These players act as the bodyguards to their favourite streamers.  
Ensuring safety whilst PvP is active, clearing nearby enemy mobs if the streamer gets "duel ganked", being ready to join dungeon runs, etc.
